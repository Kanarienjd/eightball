# Enemy Concepts

Visual source: `assets/enemy-concepts.png`

## Mask 11

Fast opener. White ball, black mask stripe, nervous eyes. Low HP, slower rhythm, teaches the player the separate rhythm battle.

## Blade 5

Aggressive mid boss. Yellow ball with diagonal black slash marking. Medium HP, tighter rhythm spacing, higher contact pressure in the arena.

## Twin 22

Trickster duo. Two connected dark balls with mirrored faces. Mid-high HP, slightly irregular-feeling rhythm pace, meant to feel like alternating attacks.

## Crazy 88

Final wave leader. Black ball with white 88 mark and red band. Highest HP, fastest rhythm, punishes missed notes hardest.
